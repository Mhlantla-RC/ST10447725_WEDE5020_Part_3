About The Odd Jobbers

Our Story

Founded in 2020, The Odd Jobbers began with a simple mission: to provide reliable, affordable moving and delivery services for everyday people. We noticed that traditional moving companies were often too expensive for small jobs, while general handyman services lacked the specialized equipment for moving tasks.

Today, we've grown into Gqeberha's most trusted service for odd jobs, combining professional moving expertise with the personal touch of local service.

Our Mission

To make moving and delivery services accessible, affordable, and stress-free for everyone in our community. We believe that everyone deserves professional help with their heavy lifting, regardless of the job size.
